This was built and tested on **Linux Mint 22.1**.
On Mint, it should run without issues.

---

If you're NOT on Linux Mint:

This program relies on:

* PipeWire
* X11 (most likely)
* AppIndicator (for tray icon)

It *should* work on other Debian-based systems, but:

* GNOME (Ubuntu) may require extra setup for tray support
* No guarantees outside Mint

---

You already made your choice. Figure it out 🤷

## Installation

Open a terminal in this folder and run:

chmod +x InstallNullWire.sh

Press enter — nothing visible will happen (that's normal).

Then run:

./InstallNullWire.sh

It may ask for your sudo password to install required dependencies.
